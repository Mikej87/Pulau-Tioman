About Pulau Tioman

Pulau Tioman is a beautiful island located off the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia. Known for its crystal-clear waters, lush rainforests, and vibrant marine life, it is a popular destination for tourists seeking adventure and relaxation.

Highlights

  • Snorkeling and diving
  • Hiking trails
  • Pristine beaches
  • Local cuisine
